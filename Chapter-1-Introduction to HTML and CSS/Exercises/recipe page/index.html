Beef Steak

This is a simple and delicious recipe for a perfectly cooked beef steak. Ideal for a special dinner!

Ingredients

  • 2 beef steaks (ribeye or sirloin)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 sprigs fresh rosemary
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Method

  1. Take the steaks out of the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature for about 30 minutes.
  2. Season both sides of the steaks generously with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat until hot.
  4. Add the steaks to the skillet and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side for medium-rare, or until desired doneness.
  5. During the last minute of cooking, add the garlic, rosemary, and butter to the skillet.
  6. Baste the steaks with the melted butter and garlic mixture.
  7. Remove the steaks from the skillet and let them rest for 5 minutes before serving.
