Grilled Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together olive oil, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, salt, and pepper.
  3. Brush the chicken breasts with the olive oil mixture.
  4. Grill the chicken for 6-8 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked.
  5. Remove from the grill and squeeze lemon juice over the chicken.
  6. Let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving.
