Spring Rolls Recipe

Ingredients

  • Rice paper wrappers
  • Vermicelli noodles
  • Shrimp, cooked and sliced in half
  • Carrots, julienned
  • Cucumber, julienned
  • Fresh mint leaves
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Leaf lettuce
  • Peanut sauce for dipping

Instructions

  1. Cook the vermicelli noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Fill a large bowl with warm water. Dip one rice paper wrapper into the water for about 5 seconds to soften.
  3. Lay the softened wrapper flat on a clean surface.
  4. Place a few pieces of shrimp, a small handful of noodles, and a few pieces of each vegetable and herbs on the bottom third of the wrapper.
  5. Fold the sides of the wrapper over the filling, then roll tightly from the bottom to the top.
  6. Repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  7. Serve the spring rolls with peanut sauce for dipping.
