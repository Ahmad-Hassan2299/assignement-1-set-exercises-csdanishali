Beef Steak Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 beef steak (about 1 inch thick)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh thyme, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or skillet to high heat.
  2. Rub the steak with olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper, rosemary, and thyme.
  3. Place the steak on the grill or skillet and cook for 4-5 minutes on each side
    for medium-rare, or until desired doneness.
  4. Remove the steak from the heat and let it rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
