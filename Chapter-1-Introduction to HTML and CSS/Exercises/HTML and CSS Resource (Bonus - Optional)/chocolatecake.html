Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 and 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 cup boiling water

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, stir together the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder,
    baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla, and mix for 2 minutes
    on medium speed of mixer.
  4. Stir in the boiling water last. The batter will be thin.
  5. Pour evenly into the prepared pans.
  6. Bake for 30 to 35 minutes in the preheated oven,
    until the cake tests done with a toothpick.
  7. Cool in the pans for 10 minutes,
    then remove to a wire rack to cool completely.
