Cheesecake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 packages (8 oz each) cream cheese, softened
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C).
  2. Mix graham cracker crumbs and melted butter in a bowl.
    Press mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.
    Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Stir in vanilla extract, sour cream, and flour until well combined.
  5. Pour mixture over crust in the springform pan.
  6. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until center is set. Turn off oven
    and let cheesecake cool in oven with door slightly open for 1 hour.
  7. Remove from oven and refrigerate for at least
    4 hours or overnight before serving.
