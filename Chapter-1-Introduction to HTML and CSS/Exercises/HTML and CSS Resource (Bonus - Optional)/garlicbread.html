Garlic Bread Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 loaf of French bread
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cut the French bread in half lengthwise.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the softened butter, minced garlic, and chopped parsley.
  4. Spread the garlic butter mixture evenly over the cut sides of the bread.
  5. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese on top of the garlic butter.
  6. Place the bread halves on a baking sheet, cut side up.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes, or until the bread is golden and crispy.
  8. Remove from the oven, let it cool slightly, then cut into slices and serve warm.
