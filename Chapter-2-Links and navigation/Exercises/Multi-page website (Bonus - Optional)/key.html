Key Information:

Kuruluş Osman is a Turkish historical drama television series, created by Mehmet Bozdağ. It focuses on the life of Osman I, the founder of the Ottoman Empire. The series is a sequel to the popular show "Diriliş: Ertuğrul" and continues the story of the Kayı tribe.

  • Genre: Historical drama, Action
  • Created by: Mehmet Bozdağ
  • Main Character: Osman Bey (played by Burak Özçivit)
  • Premiere Date: November 20, 2019
  • Language: Turkish
  • Setting: 13th-century Anatolia
  • Plot: Chronicles the life of Osman I, founder of the Ottoman Empire.
  • Prequel: Diriliş: Ertuğrul
  • Production Company: Bozdağ Film
  • Number of Seasons: 4 (as of 2023)
  • Key Themes: Leadership, bravery, justice, and faith.
  • Villains: Byzantine and Mongol forces, internal traitors.
  • Allies: Kayı tribe, loyal warriors, and family.
  • Filming Location: Riva, Istanbul
  • Music: Composed by Alpay Göktekin and Zeynep Alasya
  • Global Popularity: Widely watched in many countries.
  • Streaming Platforms: Available on various international platforms.
  • Historical Accuracy: Mix of historical facts and fiction.
  • Legacy: Highlights the rise of the Ottoman Empire.
  • Awards: Won several national and international awards.
